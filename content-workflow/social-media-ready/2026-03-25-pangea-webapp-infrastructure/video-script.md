# Video Script — PANGEA: R Shiny vs Production Web App (~20 seconds)

## Visual Flow

**0–4s** — Screen recording of R Shiny app
> "This is the original R Shiny prototype."

**4–8s** — Show lag, clunky UI, or a slow interaction in Shiny
> "It worked. But it wasn't built for real clinical use."

**8–12s** — Cut to pangeamodels.org loading instantly
> "So we rebuilt it."

**12–18s** — Enter patient values, show progression curve rendering smoothly
> "Same model. Same predictions. But fast, reliable, and available to any clinician worldwide."

**18–20s** — Hold on the final risk output screen
> "Published in Nature Medicine. Free at pangeamodels.org."
