## Design Specifications

**Dimensions:** 1080x1080px (Instagram square) or 1200x630px (LinkedIn/social)

**Brand Colors:**
- Use your consistent brand colors
- Ensure high contrast for readability

**Typography:**
- Quote text: Large, readable sans-serif font
- Attribution: Smaller, lighter weight

**Layout:**
- Quote centered or left-aligned
- Attribution at bottom
- Your logo or website subtle watermark
- Keep background simple (solid color or subtle gradient)

**Accessibility:**
- Text should pass WCAG contrast requirements
- Font size large enough to read on mobile
